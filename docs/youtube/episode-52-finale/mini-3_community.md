## How to Get Help

### 1. Search First

- Google your error message
- Check Stack Overflow
- Read documentation

### 2. Ask Good Questions

## Networking Tips

1. **Be genuine** - Don't just network to "get something"
2. **Share knowledge** - Teach what you know
3. **Engage regularly** - Consistency builds relationships
4. **Attend events** - In-person connections are powerful
5. **Follow up** - Keep in touch with people you meet
